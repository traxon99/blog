Welcome to my blog. I've wanted a place to write down things I'm learning,
working on, or just find interesting — so here it is.

## what to expect

Posts will mostly be technical: software, programming languages, projects I'm
hacking on. Occasionally something else might sneak in. No fixed schedule,
just whenever I have something worth writing.

## the setup

This blog is plain HTML, CSS, and Markdown. No frameworks, no build step, no dependencies.

To add a new post:

1. Create `posts/your-slug.md` with a front matter block at the top
2. Add a line to the list in `index.html`

That's it.
